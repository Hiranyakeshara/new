Learn - About Us


La Boutique Del Rammendo is a pioneer in laundry and mending services in Milan and boasts 40 years of experience in the service. We understand that first impression talks all about you, and that is why we are dedicated to helping you look and feel the best with our washing, pressing, stitching and dry cleaning services. At La Boutique Del Rammendo each garment is individually evaluated in order to determine the safest and the most effective methods of service. We provide state of the art cleaning process that ensures the fabrics are handled gently and the stains are treated hard. Tailoring services is another major aspect of La Boutiques service portfolio.
